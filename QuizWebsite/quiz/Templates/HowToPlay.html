How to Play

The goal of the quiz is to get as many right answers out of 10 as you can.
The quiz has four levels of difficulties corresponding to a different school group.
Each level has its own categories that will test you about that topic.
There will be 4 choices available for each question and you can only pick one.
If you answer correctly, the choice will light up green and you will receive one point.
If you answer incorrectly, the choice will light up red and you will receive no points.
At the end, you will be shown your score and you can decide if you want to play again or go back to the main menu.
